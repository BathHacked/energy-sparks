Energy Sparks supports schools in becoming more energy-efficient by providing better access to their energy usage data. It uses a simple point system, and healthy competition between schools, to motivate all members of the school community to work together to save energy.

By presenting usage data in easy-to-use and understand ways, teachers, pupils, governors and parents can be all be better informed.

By making it easier to use energy usage data in the classroom, Energy Sparks supports schools in integrating energy issues into the curriculum. The data, along with the school's record of energy-saving activities, helps schools to achieve Eco-Schools awards.

For feedback and questions, please email hello@energysparks.uk. Or contact us on social media
